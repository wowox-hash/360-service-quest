## Encounter 1: Brussels, Belgium
*(Segment C)*

**Distributor:** Marc Peeters · Purchasing Manager
**Company:** AutoParts Benelux
**Channel:** 220 garages + 15 fast-fit centres
**Rewards:** +50g · +120 XP

### Opening speech (NPC intro)
> Thank you for coming. We have a serious problem with product recommendations. Our 15 counter staff use printed guides last updated six months ago. Hybrids, Euro 7, Chinese imports — our team is guessing. Garages are sending products back. We're losing €8,000/month in reverse logistics. Three garages told us last month they're considering switching. I need a solution that works at the counter, every day.

### Discovery phase
**Situational prompt:** _Marc is losing €8,000/month to returns. You have one discovery question before the pitch — make it count._

**Discovery questions the salesperson can ask (only ONE is correct):**

**Option A** ✅ _correct — bonus damage_
- Question: When your counter staff get a vehicle they're not sure about, walk me through exactly what happens today.
- NPC response: Marc: "They check the printed guide. If it's not listed, they call the hotline or just pick the closest match." — That's the gap. The outdated guide is shipping wrong products.

**Option B** ⚠️ _wrong pillar — enemy buff_
- Question: Would upselling every garage to a premium synthetic that covers most applications solve it?
- NPC response: Marc: "That might protect quality, but it doesn't solve the identification problem." — You pitched product before discovery. He's now defensive.

**Option C** ➖ _shallow — neutral_
- Question: Do your staff just ask the garage what they used last time?
- NPC response: Marc: "Sometimes — but that just perpetuates old errors." — A shallow probe. You learned something, but not the root cause.

### Battle objections

**Objection 1** (CONNECT pillar · 30 HP)
- Text: ""My counter staff don't have time for another tool. They handle 200+ calls daily.""
- Weakness (super-effective services): reco_tool
- Resistance (wrong-pillar / ineffective): oil_analysis, trade_fairs

**Objection 2** (CONNECT pillar · 34 HP)
- Text: ""We already have TecDoc through our parts platform. How is yours different?""
- Weakness (super-effective services): tecdoc
- Resistance (wrong-pillar / ineffective): marketing_portal, promo_calendar

**Objection 3** (CONNECT pillar · 26 HP)
- Text: ""How do I know this actually reduces returns? I need proof, not promises.""
- Weakness (super-effective services): tecdoc, reco_tool
